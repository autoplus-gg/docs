## Server Rules [#rules]

Before you start using the AutoPlus Discord server, please read and understand the following rules. Failure to comply with these rules may result in a warning, mute, or ban from the server.

<ol className="list-decimal list-inside ml-4 space-y-2">
  <li>
    Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, sexism, racism, hate speech, or any
    form of discriminatory behavior will not be tolerated.
  </li>
  <li>
    No spam or unsolicited self-promotion. This includes advertising in the
    server or directly messaging members without permission.
  </li>
  <li>No age-restricted, obscene, or otherwise inappropriate content.</li>
  <li>
    Messages in community channels must be sent in English. Messages in other
    languages may be removed.
  </li>
  <li>
    AutoPlus maintains a neutral moderation approach and does not apply scammer
    markings directly on the server. Marked roles are handled by third-party
    services such as <a href="https://discord.gg/scammeralert">ScammerAlert</a>{" "}
    and <a href="https://discord.gg/ksf">KSF</a> through the "@Repcord" bot.
    These roles are visual only and do not affect AutoPlus services,
    transactions, or dispute outcomes in any way.
  </li>
  <li>
    Messages related to scams, fraudulent activity, or content that violates
    Discord's Terms of Service may be deleted.
  </li>
</ol>

## Exchange Channel [#exchange-channel]

The exchange channel is made for people who are looking for an exchange. It is not a place for advertisements, casual chatting, or non-English messages. Posts that do not follow this can be deleted and may lead to a sanction.

You can select exchange roles from the pinned message in the exchange channel. These roles let other members mention you when they are looking for a specific payment method, such as Bank, PayPal, CashApp, Revolut, Apple Pay, Crypto to Crypto, Gift Card, or AutoPlus Points.

Do not spam exchange role mentions. If you mention exchange roles too often, AutoPlus Helper warns you on the second mention and applies a 10-minute chat restriction on the third mention within 10 minutes.

## Sanctions [#sanctions]

AutoPlus uses moderation cases to track warnings and mutes. For common rule breaks, staff can use auto-sanctioning so repeated issues escalate in a consistent way.

Auto-sanctions escalate separately for each reason category over a rolling 90-day period. For example, advertising in the exchange channel and advertising in another channel are both treated as advertising, while a non-English message has a separate history. Staff-written custom reasons share a separate custom category.

The first level is usually a warning. Repeated cases in the same category can then become mutes of `5m`, `1h`, `24h`, `7d`, and finally `30d`. Cases older than 90 days, deleted cases, and unmute or unban records do not increase the level. The automatic 10-minute restriction for excessive exchange-role mentions also does not increase later auto-sanctions. Cooldown safeguards prevent several cases recorded close together from skipping through multiple levels at once.

When possible, AutoPlus sends the user a direct message with the reason and, for mutes, when the mute ends. Staff can still choose stronger action for serious issues such as scams, threats, or severe rule violations.

## Message Cleanup [#message-cleanup]

Some public server channels are automatically cleaned to keep them readable. Messages older than 24 hours may be deleted from these channels.
