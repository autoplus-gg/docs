The points system is AutoPlus's virtual currency designed to provide additional fun ways to engage with our bot and community. Points can be earned through trades, referrals, and special events, then used for various activities and games within our ecosystem.

## How to earn points [#how-to-earn-points]

You can earn points when you do trades using AutoPlus. Both the sender and receiver will get 5 points each for every $ of escrow fee paid. For example, for a $100 trade with $0.25 escrow fee, both the sender and receiver will get 1.25 points each.

You can also earn points when referring people to AutoPlus. You will get 10 points for each $ of escrow fee paid by the person you referred. [Click here to learn more about the referral system](/docs/referral-system).

Boosting the official AutoPlus Discord server is another way to earn points. When you boost the server, you will instantly receive `🔹10` points.

Additionally, every Monday at midnight CET/CEST, three random server boosters are selected to receive `🔹10` points each as a thank you for their support.

## How to use points [#how-to-use-points]

For the moment, you can only use points to play games like coinflip, blackjack, plink and mines. If you have any suggestions on what we should add, feel free to contact us.

You can send points to other people by using the command `/tip`. 1% of the amount you tip will be burnt to reduce the inflation of the points.

## How to check your points [#how-to-check-points]

There are two ways to check your points. You can either use the command `/balance` or you can check your points with the command `/stats user` to see your points or the points of another user.
