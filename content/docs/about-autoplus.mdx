## What is AutoPlus [#what-is-autoplus]

AutoPlus is a automated cryptocurrency escrow service on Discord established in June 2023. Our mission is to provide secure escrow services to **EVERYONE**, which is why we try to ban as few people as possible in order to provide our services to as many people as possible. The escrow system is fully automated, ensuring the fastest transaction detection and the fastest confirmation detection. We also have a support team to help you if you have any questions or problems (like a dispute). We are always looking for ways to improve our service, so if you have any suggestions, feel free to contact us.

## Why should I trust AutoPlus [#why-should-i-trust-autoplus]

The private keys of the escrow wallets are accessible only by a single person, reducing the risk of an exit scam to one person rather than a group of people.

## What are the fees [#what-are-the-fees]

The escrow fees are 0.25% of the amount being held in escrow. With **no minimum fee** or trade amount, reflecting our commitment to accessibility.

## Rules and policies [#rules-and-policies]

Before using AutoPlus, review the [Server Rules](/docs/server-guide#rules) and [Escrow Rules](/docs/trade-system#escrow-rules). The Escrow Rules explain user responsibilities, eligible trades, dispute response times, and how seller terms are handled.
