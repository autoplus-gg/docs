AutoPlus Helper adds small Discord tools that make trades easier to manage without leaving the server. These tools are meant for quick checks and calculations during conversations.

## Crypto Address Checker [#crypto-address-checker]

When you send a Bitcoin, Litecoin, or Ethereum address inside `#bot-commands`, AutoPlus Helper can reply with public blockchain information about that address.

The reply may show the address balance, transaction count, total sent, total received, and an estimated USD value. This is useful when you want to quickly inspect an address before or during a trade.

## Fiat Currency Converter [#fiat-currency-converter]

You can type a fiat conversion directly in Discord, such as `10 USD to EUR`, `$25 to GBP`, or `100 EUR in USD`. AutoPlus Helper replies with the converted amount.

This is especially useful inside escrow threads when both traders use different currencies and need a quick estimate.

## Calculator Replies [#calculator-replies]

AutoPlus Helper can also answer simple calculations written in chat, such as `250 + 15`, `100 - 10%`, or `50 times 3`.

This helps when calculating totals, discounts, split payments, or fees inside escrow threads without opening another calculator.
