## Staff impersonation [#staff-impersonation]

AutoPlus staff will **NEVER** ask for your Discord token, password, or any other sensitive information. If someone claims to be an AutoPlus staff member and asks for this information, they are impersonating a staff member and should be reported.<br />
Staff members will **NEVER** send you a DM to release or return the funds.<br />
If someone claiming to be staff messages you directly, do not engage with them and report them immediately.
